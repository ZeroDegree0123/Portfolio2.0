PlanIt

Planit is here to track all those tedious tasks you've been stressing to get on top of. PlanIt is easy to use and updates you on your current tasks at hand. PlanIt also makes a list of all the tasks you complete making it easy to track your progress and reviewing what you did in the current day. And best of all, PlanIt is completely free... Thats right, free!

PlanIt is a mobile task app built using React Native, Tailwind and firebase. The app can track current and completed tasks. The app has full secure athentication functionality, users can easily sign-up and Login to their accounts. This app was made just for fun and to dive into new technologies and test out new styles.

Future plans for PlanIt are ongoing. Additions to come are a calendar page, user customization, and push notifications.
